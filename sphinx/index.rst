HOLOR
=============

   .. image:: ./images/holor_logo.png

Holor is a C++ template, header-only library that implements generic multi-dimensional containers. The name Holor was inspired by the work of Parry Moon and Domina Eberle Spencer

   A holor ist ...
   
   --- Parry Moon and Domina Eberle Spencer (Theory of Holors: a generalization of tensors)
